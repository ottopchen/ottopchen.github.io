About Us

The first Buffet Fortuna was opened in 1990 in San Leandro, California by Tony Fong. As a first generation immigrant, Tony took his expertise in cooking and created a buffet serving a multitude of dishes that combine Asian and American culture. In 2005, he opened a second buffet in Downtown Oakland, and continues to serve thousands of customers on a weekly basis.
